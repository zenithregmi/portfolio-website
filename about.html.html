About Us: St. Mary's Co.Ed School

Welcome to St. Mary's Co.Ed School, a renowned educational institution with a remarkable history that began in the year 2050 B.S. Founded by the visionary Mr. Navraj Gurung, our school has been a cornerstone of academic excellence and character development for decades.

Our Founder: Mr. Navraj Gurung ,a trailblazer with a deep passion for education, envisioned a place where young minds could flourish, where knowledge and values would intertwine to create responsible citizens and future leaders. His unwavering dedication and commitment to quality education have laid the foundation for our school's success.

Our Principal: Mr. Paramananda Regmi Under the dynamic leadership of our esteemed principal, Mr. Paramananda Regmi, St. Mary's Co.Ed School has grown into an institution that not only imparts knowledge but also nurtures creativity, leadership, and social responsibility. Mr. Regmi's vision and guidance continue to inspire our students and staff to reach new heights.

St. Mary's School holds a significant place in the educational landscape of Birendranagar-03, Surkhet. Established in the year 2050 BS, the school has a rich history of providing quality education and fostering holistic development among its students. Situated in the heart of the town, St. Mary's School has become a cornerstone of academic excellence and character building within the local community. With a vision to empower young minds and prepare them for the challenges of the modern world, St. Mary's School has consistently upheld its commitment to providing a well-rounded education. The school's curriculum goes beyond textbooks, aiming to nurture critical thinking, creativity, and effective communication skills among its students. This approach equips students not only with academic knowledge but also with the tools to thrive in a dynamic and rapidly changing society. One of the school's notable strengths is its dedicated faculty. The teachers at St. Mary's School are not just educators but also mentors who guide and inspire students to reach their full potential. Their unwavering dedication to their profession and genuine concern for the well-being of the students have created a supportive and nurturing learning environment. Beyond academics, St. Mary's School places great importance on extracurricular activities. The school believes in the holistic development of students, encouraging them to explore their talents and interests outside the classroom. From sports to arts, from debate clubs to community service initiatives, students have a plethora of opportunities to discover their passions and develop essential life skills. Situated in Birendranagar-03, St. Mary's School plays an integral role in the local community. It serves as a center of education and culture, bringing together students from diverse backgrounds to learn, grow, and share experiences. The school's commitment to fostering a sense of community and social responsibility is evident through its various outreach programs and collaborations with local organizations. In conclusion, St. Mary's School, established in 2050 BS, stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Birendranagar-03, Surkhet. Through its unwavering dedication to academic rigor, character development, and extracurricular engagement, the school continues to shape the future of its students and contribute positively to the overall development of the region
